FAQ

Good questions

Do you negotiate or buy the car for me? +

No — we're an advisory service. We help you choose the right car with total confidence; you handle the actual purchase. (A full concierge negotiation tier may come later.)

What happens after I pay? +

You'll get your car survey by email. Once you complete it, we book your call or chat and start building your report — and a copy of your survey lands in your inbox automatically.

New, used, or both? +

Both — we recommend based on your budget, priorities, and the package you choose. Often the smartest pick is a lightly-used version of a newer model.

Which package is right for me? +

Torn between two specific cars? Start with Gut Check. Shopping more broadly? Full Service covers all your options. Need to plan multiple vehicles for the household? Go Whole Garage.
