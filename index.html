FAQ

Good questions

Do you assist with the car buying process? +

No — we are not a car concierge service. We do not hunt down specific cars and negotiate deals for you. We help on the front end of the car shopping process when you are figuring out what cars fit you best. (Note: we can recommend other car buying services at the end of your consultation if that is a service you are interested in.)

What happens after I pay? +

You'll get your car survey by email. Once you complete it, we book your call or chat and start building your report — and a copy of your survey lands in your inbox automatically.

New, used, or both? +

We will gladly recommend both new and used vehicles based on your preferences, budget, priorities, etc. If you are specifically shopping new or specifically shopping pre-owned vehicles, you can let us know in your survey.
