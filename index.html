FAQ

Good questions

Do you negotiate or buy the car for me? +

No — we're an advisory service. We find the right cars for your situation and arm you with the reasoning; you handle the actual purchase. (A full concierge negotiation tier may come later.)

New, used, or both? +

Both — we recommend based on your budget and priorities, and the package you choose. Often the smartest pick is a lightly-used version of a new model.

How long does it take? +

Most match reports go out within 3–5 business days of your completed survey. Need it faster? Mention it and we'll see what's possible.

What if I don't love the picks? +

The Match and The Driveway include revision rounds — we refine the shortlist with your feedback until it clicks.
