# Fix: automatically send customers a copy of their survey

**Problem:** When a customer submits the Tally survey, Adam gets the response but the customer doesn't — Adam has to email a copy manually.

**Fix:** Turn on Tally's respondent email notification with the answers attached as a PDF. One-time setup, then it's automatic forever. This also makes good on the promise already written into the survey intro ("you will receive a copy of your survey").

> Note: attaching the PDF is a **Tally Pro** feature (~$29/mo). The form already collects the customer's email (question 2), so nothing needs to be added to the form itself.

## Steps (in Adam's Tally account)

1. Open the **Full Service Survey** form in Tally.
2. Go to the form's **Settings → Emails** (also reachable via **Integrations → Email notifications**).
3. Find **"Respondent email notifications"** and turn it **on**.
4. Set the **recipient** to the form's **Email** field (the email question).
5. Write a short confirmation message (suggested copy below).
6. Turn on **"Attach PDF with answers"** so the customer gets a clean PDF of everything they submitted.
7. **Save**, and make sure the form is **published**.
8. Test it: submit the form yourself with your own email and confirm the copy + PDF arrive.

Exact click-by-click with screenshots: https://tally.so/help/respondent-email-notifications

## Suggested confirmation email copy

> **Subject:** Your Like Your Car survey — copy enclosed
>
> Hi [first name],
>
> Thanks for completing your Like Your Car survey! A copy of your answers is attached as a PDF for your records.
>
> I'll review your responses and be in touch within 48 hours to set up your call or chat. In the meantime, if anything changes or you think of something you forgot to mention, just reply to this email.
>
> Talk soon,
> Adam — Like Your Car

## Repeat for the other tiers

If Gut Check and Whole Garage use separate Tally forms, repeat these steps on each of those forms too.
